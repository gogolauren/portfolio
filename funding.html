District of Columbia

Resident from District of Columbia may be eligible for the following RiverSmart rebates:


  • Rain barrel rebate
  • Tree rebate
  • Landscaping Rebates


to see requirements and the maximum rebate, click and check Riversmart website



Prince George's County

Resident from Prince George's County may be eligible for the following RiverSmart rebates:


  • Cisterns
  • Rain roofs
  • Pavement removal
  • Permeable pavement
  • Rain barrels
  • Rain gardens
  • Urban tree canopy


to see requirements and the maximum rebate, click and check Rain Check website
